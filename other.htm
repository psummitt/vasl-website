Other Boards & CG Maps

Status Update: December, 2023

Since VASL has automated the board update process, there is no longer a need for players themselves to download boards, even new boards as they are issued. You will always have access to the latest version of any VASL-ized board, just select it from the board picker in VASL!


Due to the success of the automated board update process, this webpage is no longer being updated and will be removed entirely in early 2024.


It was intended to leave this page as an archive of VASL boards. However, continued requests for changes to the page make that unfeasible.
